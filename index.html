Core Music began with a simple idea: to
celebrate the diversity of music.

I wanted to create a space where every genre,
from jazz to hip-hop, could coexist
harmoniously. The welcome screen captures
this spirit, with images of musicians from
different genres and backgrounds.

The neon Rolling Stones logo serves as a
tribute to the timeless influence of music
legends, symbolizing the bridge detween the
past and the future.

Core Music is more than a platform; it's a
celebration of the world's musical tapestry.
